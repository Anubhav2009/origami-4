How to make a camel origami

1.Concave a small portion of the blind corner. Raise the bottom corner.

2.Concave corners. Lower the corner.

3.Bending the side to the middle, flatten your pocket. Make an incision. When bending, lower the corner.

4.In the lower corners make double folds zippers. Concave the corner at the top. Bend the corners to the right with the mountain. Concave angle-tail.

5.Concave corners on hind limbs, bend corners with a mountain. On the forelimbs, double fold the zippers. Bend the corner-head to the left.

6.Take the corner inside the head. Bend the corners on the hind limbs. Opening the corner, lift it up.

8.Fold in half.

9.Bend your ears, stretching the corners. Make double folds of zippers around your neck, pulling your neck to the side. Bend the corner-tail. Decorate the limbs.

10.Glue for crease on the extremities. Set the figure.
